During the tour you’ll be listening, viewing, tasting, and admiring the historical and cultural values of North-Western region of the country. By the countryside paths, village roads and railways we’ll reach the sites of important historical events and famous persons, the sites, where they still cook delicious dishes and drinks! From the history and culture reserve “Zaslaŭje” you’ll go the wide-known in the past Jewish town of Haradok, where you’ll be hosted at local potters’ residence. In the morning you’ll go further to touch the walls of the legendary Kreva castle or to come around Michal Kleafas Ahinski’s noble estate for a dance based on his melody...
