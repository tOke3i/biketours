This is a trip to the Karališčavičy picturesque village, which carpets high banks of the Svislač river and still keeps memory of the native noble culture, the poetry and the old cuisine… Apart this, the tour includes a visit to a site of former Nazi’s extermination camp and a visit of one of the most ambient industrial sites, so called the construction cranes cemetery…

Sites: ruins of the Prušynskis manor house - st. Stanislaŭ Roman catholic church (XVIII cent.) - poet Jazep Pušča’s tomb - the old cemetery (XIX cent.) - art center - the construction cranes cemetery - the “Gates of Memory” memorial
