Great history of the exemplary socialist settlement mixed with small stories of its constructors and inhabitants in order to become an entire narrative. Constructed first in the post-war Minsk by German prisoners of war and Belarusian villagers, it became a symbol of Belarusian capital's rebirth, a symbol of giving back of the “urbanity” of the Belarusians, a model for constructing other soviet industrial settlements, nice and cosy home for a number of soviet families, an ambient décor for motion picture filming. All the seventy years of its crash workdays and celebrations are in the walking tour!

Sites: Minsk Tractor Works (main entrance), Lenin's monument, sport grounds (including a ground for the game "Gorodki"), fountain "Tree of life", small-scale sculpture of 1950s, church of John Baptizer's Birth, former Pioneers garden, front buildings of the main street, public institutions, houses of 1945-50s, House of soviet top performers, "House with mermaids", house for officers-pilots (1940), Antonijeŭski garden, former and modern Palaces of Culture of Minsk Tractor Works (1940 and 1950-s by Georgi Bieniedyktaŭ) etc.
