Minsk nonconformists from the “Bielaruski klimat” art-community were bored in the exemplary Soviet capital in the end of 1980s. That is why they decided to populate ghosts, dragons and knights here, imaging castle ruins and mystery signs everywhere around… Evening walkings with outrageous tricks, endless imagination around the main city sites became a real tour, which filled the city with a new mysterious and ironic sense, explained its name, origins and space in a totally other manner. In the Soviet time for such stories one could be beaten by boneheads, caught by the police, put into a nut hospital. But now we’re glad to invite you to the tour, dear amateur of the city, the humour and adventures.

Sites: Underground, the fourth house of the third Russian Labor Socialist Party meeting, Mienisk’s phallus, Mienisk’s testis, the place of sailing toy boats, site of Mienisk’s castle, a little birch and a little asp near the Communist Party Central Committee, the last axolotl
