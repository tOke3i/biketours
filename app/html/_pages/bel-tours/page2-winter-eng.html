The tour around the famous Lošycki park is in an unusual manner! While someone is carping about the coldness and Sun absence, we’ll obtain healthy ruddiness on our cheeks, get warm by quick moves, admire the beauty of the winter park and listen to stories of old Lošyca.

Sites: park’s flora, the count Liubanski manor house and outhouse (XIX cent.), old mills and distillery, ruins of the Crypt Chapel of the Prušynskis etc. After the tour the visit of the museum “The estate of Lošyca” is posible (at a cost).
