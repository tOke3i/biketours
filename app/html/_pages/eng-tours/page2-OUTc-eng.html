Up and down, up and down again! From the space height to the green valley! The trip is along an “olympic” suburb cycle path, around forests and fields, along a comfortable cycle track of the most “cycle” district of the city. Between the paths there are key-points of our turbo speed-ups:

  • the museum of aircrafts with a number of airplanes, helicopters and an “cosmic” exposition - everything is to remind that little boy who wanted to be a cosmonaut!
  • Olympic training center in Raubičy village with its world-known ups and downs, start and finish.
To come back to the Earth, we’ll visit a fine museum of the Belarusian folk crafts which surprises by its miracles made from straw, clay, wood, linen, collected under a spire of an old church that stands on a beautiful romantic hill. At the end, we’ll have a promenade around a premium cottage settlement, with a chance to stop for a while at a beach or at a local cafe...

Additional costs:

  • bicycle rental (if needed)
