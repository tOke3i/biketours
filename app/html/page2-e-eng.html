They are touching the sky, but they are staying confidently on our roofs. The city raised them up, appointed them as mediators. This is an authors tour about the inhabitants of the roofs of Minsk, who are towering on the main buildings’ facades, you should be ready to hold your head up!

Sites - monumental sculptures on the roofs of: Suvorov military school (XIX cent., 1953), National Opera and Ballet Theatre (1934, 2010s), an house on vul.Čyrvonaja (1956), Belarusian National Technical Academy’s hostel (1952-56), Minsk state architectural and construction college (1954), Main statistic department of Minsk region (1954), Palace of culture of the professional unions (1954), National arts museum (1957), GUM (Main department store, 1951), the building of the Ministry of Internal Affairs (1913), Belarusian state teacher training university (1946), City Gate (1954, 2000s)
