Auschwitz, Treblinka, Kurapaty are well-known sites of sorrow. Trascianiec was a big death factory of 1930-40s of the same calibre, but it was almost forgotten. Today there one could see numerous memorials on the sites of massacres: great official ones and non formal “screams” of relatives about their loss. Here a giant number of life journeys finished, the people were trying to find a way to a better life, but they found Way to foreverness only…

Sites: prisoners reception’s place, Soviet memorial (1963), massacres sites and memorials, place of the cremator, remains of household buildings of the extermination camp, the “Gates of Memory” memorial
