About us

locals, we like our city

мы интересуемся нашим городом и страной, ценим их и постоянно стремимся узнать их глубже

certified guides

all of us were certified as guides at the National tourism agency of the Republic of Belarus, all of us have blue-badges; all of us have experience to make the tours

no hidden tricks

we don’t lead tourists to souvenir shops, cafes etc. for hidden profits, we could only recommend good places that we like

since 2014

since that time we made hundreds of the tours for all kinds of tourist groups, including ambassadors, bureaucrats, showmen, IT-workers, railmen and many other good people.

speak languages

at the moment we speak Belarusian, English, Polish, Russian, French

not only beaten tracks

besides beautiful city center, we also like backstreets and nooks of our city, districts with old wooden houses, industrial areas, small gardens etc.; we like to share our discoveries with those who have the same interests

authorial manner

we explore the city in different ways, discovering new sites, which then become a base for new authorial tours that we can make for you
