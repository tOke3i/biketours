The tour is around picturesque sites of Northern-West Minsk region, along a high riverbank of the Minska-Viliejskaja water system. Old churches, ruined and alive ones, the pieces of the noble culture of the past, hydrologic constructions of the Soviet period, a site of a medieval castle and Belarusian cultural luminaries’ sites (e.g. Janka Kupala, Branislaŭ Taraškievič, Aliaksandr Ulasaŭ, Jadvihin Š.), the place of the heroic act of Mikalaj Hastela, - everything will fill the tour with the spirit of greatness of the Belarusian history.

Additional costs:
  • local trains tickets or escort vehicle
  • museum entrance fee and museum tour guide service (at option)
  • visit of the ceramics factory of Radaškovičy (at option)
  • bicycle rental (if needed)

Sites:

  • Dubrava: ruins of the Virgin Mary Roman catholic church (XVIII cent.), distillery (ХІХ cent.), Water pump station of Minska-Viliejskaja water system;
  • Radaškovičy: st.Trinity Roman catholic church (XІХ cent.), st. Elijah Orthodox church (1942), house of Branislaŭ Taraškievič, Janka Kupala monument, Mikalaj Hastela monument, ceramic factory, site of the castle etc.;
  • Viazynka: the Zembryckis noble estate (Janka Kupala memorial reserve);
